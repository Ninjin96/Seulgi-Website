Who is Seulgi?

Member of Red Velvet.

Kang Seul-gi (Korean: 강슬기), is the Main Dancer and Lead Vocalist of the South Korean girl group Red Velvet.

Birthday.

Seulgi was born on February 10, 1994 in Ansan, South Korea.

Relationship status.

Seulgi is currently dating Ninjin Tuan (Korean: 퇀닌진).
